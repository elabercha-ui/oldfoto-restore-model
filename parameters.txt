SLIDERS (numeric inputs)

• face_resemblance: 0–1  
  Controls how closely the restored face matches the original.

• detail_resemblance: 0–1  
  Controls how close the texture/details remain to the original.

• face_deformity: 0–1  
  Reduces unnatural distortions in the face.

• detail_deformity: 0–1  
  Reduces unnatural distortions in textures and small details.

• smoothness: 0–100  
  Smoothing strength (higher = softer skin and background).

• noise_level: 0–1  
  Noise reduction strength.

• flux_impact: 0–1  
  Weight of FLUX-based enhancement.

• upscale_level: 2–8  
  Upscale multiplier.

• color_levels: 0–1  
  Adjusts tonal correction strength.

• blur_levels: 0–1  
  Blur reduction or sharpening balance.
